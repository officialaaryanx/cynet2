CYNET 2026: Annual IT Fest

Date: 2026-03-22

Innovating for a Sustainable Tomorrow Through Technology

Jagannath International Management School successfully hosted CYNET 2026, an exhilarating IT Fest on March 22, 2026, at its Vasant Kunj campus. The event saw enthusiastic participation from 300+ students representing colleges from across Delhi and the NCR region, making it a vibrant and competitive platform for young tech enthusiasts.

The fest featured an array of tech-based competitions, workshops, and interactive sessions focused on emerging technologies like AI, UX design, and sustainable IT solutions. Participants had the opportunity to showcase their technical expertise, enhance problem-solving skills, and collaborate on innovative solutions for a more sustainable future.

Students engaged in exciting competitions that tested their creativity and critical thinking, making CYNET 2025 a true celebration of technology and sustainability. The event also provided a valuable platform for networking with industry experts, gaining insights into future tech trends, and preparing for real-world challenges.

To honor the talent and hard work displayed by participants, winners of various competitions were awarded exciting prizes, recognizing their innovation, problem-solving skills, and technical expertise. CYNET 2025 celebrated not just competition but also the spirit of learning and excellence in technology.
